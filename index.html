About Us

English Now Course (ENC) helps you talk to the world, in English and other widely spoken languages. We are committed to assisting the government in educating the nation to be able to compete with the outside world.

We have a lot of audio and video material for language practice. This material includes stories from a podcast, an audio soap opera, a series of English teaching TV programs produced with the BBC and videos showing how to say things correctly in very different situations.
